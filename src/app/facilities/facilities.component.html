Sai Engineering College

Facilities

Hostel Accommodation

We provide comfortable and safe hostel accommodation for our students. Our hostels are equipped with all necessary amenities to ensure a pleasant stay.

Physical Education

Our college emphasizes the importance of physical education. We have well-equipped sports facilities and encourage students to participate in various sports activities.

Transport

We offer transport facilities for students and staff. Our buses cover various routes to ensure convenient transportation to and from the college.

Dispensary

We have a dispensary on campus to provide basic medical assistance to students and staff. Our trained medical staff ensures prompt and efficient healthcare services.

Ambulance

In case of emergencies, we have an ambulance service available 24/7 to provide immediate medical assistance and transport to nearby hospitals if needed.

Food Court

Our college campus has a food court offering a variety of nutritious and delicious food options. We ensure hygienic food preparation and a comfortable dining environment.

Bank

We have a bank branch located on campus to cater to the banking needs of students and staff. The bank provides various services, including ATM facilities.

Post Office

For the convenience of our students and staff, we have a post office situated within the college premises. It offers postal and courier services.
